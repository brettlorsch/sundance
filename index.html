Popular activities in the area

Skiing/Snowboarding

Utah is known for having the greatest snow on earth. Durring your stay you will be just minutes from the resort, so you can injoy the great snow and have the ability to take breaks throughout the day to warm up in the cabin next to the fire place.

You will also be a short 30 minute drive to Park City where you have access to Dear Valley Resort and Park City Mountain Resort. You will have plenty of options to have an amazing time durring your stay here at Sundance.

Hiking

Sundance resort has over 10 miles of hiking trails, with the option of riding the chair lift up and taking the trail down. If you feel like getting more of a workout, you could hike up and take the chair lift down, or even hike both directions.

You also have hundereds of additional trails right outside of the resort. There are trails for all skill levels. If you want and advanced hike you can take the timpooneke trail to the top of the mountain (15 miles round trip)

Mountain Biking

Sundance offers over 25 miles of lift-serviced mountain biking, trails. All Sundance trails are designed for uphill and downhill, feature challenging terrain and breathtaking views and range from intermediate to advanced trail riding. If you are new to mountain biking, they offer courses at their Mt. Biking School to get you up and riding safely in no time.

Can't bring your bike with you? No need to worry. They also offer Mt. bike rentals for your convience.

Horse Back Riding

Do you want to see some wild life and experience the beauty of the Wasatch Mountains? If so, the Sundance horse back riding tour may be just what your looking for. All rides are either private groups or 6 no more than 6 people. So you know you will have a wonderfull time without the hastle of a large group of people.

If you want to make sure you are able to take advantage of this durring your stay click here to make your reservation.

Fly Fishing

Just five minutes from Sundance Mountain Resort you’ll find one of the premier Blue Ribbon trout fisheries in the world: the Provo River. The Provo River is known for its large German Brown Trout, easy wading access, prolific hatches and stunning beauty. These features combine to create a world-class fishing experience any time of the year.

Dining

If you're the type of person who enjoys fine dining, you have come to the right place. At Sundance's Tree Room Resturant you will find upscale dining with serene mountain views.

You also have a variety of casual resturants at the resort and a short 15 minute drive down to Provo.

Zipline

Experience Sundance Mountain Resort and the breathtaking views of Mount Timpanogos from the sky! The Sundance ZipTour is one of the most exciting and scenic zip tours in the world, boasting over 2,100 feet of vertical drop — the most of any zip line tour in the United States. Guests can ride side-by-side on the zip line’s double cables and control their speed, cruising over 65 miles per hour, or stopping mid-air to take in the views.

Snow Shoing

The Sundance Nordic Center offers 15 kilometers of groomed cross country ski trails for both classic and skating techniques. The Nordic Center has developed a reputation for the quality of the grooming operations which occur on a nightly basis leaving untouched trails each morning with classic tracks and a wide skating lane. The trail network is laid out through pristine back country terrain creating a unique experience for all ability levels.
